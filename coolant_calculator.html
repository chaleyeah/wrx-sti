Build Notes
FSM spec is 8.1 qt. Radiator dropdown figures are estimates: anchored on Koyo (~+1.5 qt over stock → 9.6 qt) and scaled by added core thickness (stock ~27mm, IAG 42mm, Koyo 53mm, IAG 55mm). PWR varies by model. These guide how much fluid to buy — always fill to level marks, not by volume. Burp with nose elevated. Change annually at 70/30 — more frequently below 25% glycol. VP Cool Down is added last after system is filled and running.
